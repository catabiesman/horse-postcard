## Browse Postcards
Travellers often remarked on the beauty of the places or vistas of the locations they were writing from. Some authors even acknowledged the receipt of photographs that had been sent to them by the postcard's intended recipient suggesting 1. an ongoing practice of correspondence between these actors, and 2. the rising accessibility and interest in photography in the early 20th century. Refer to postcards 11, 15, and 22.
